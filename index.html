Le Anh Practical Accounting Training Center is known as one of the leading prestigious accounting training facilities in Vietnam. Introducing Le Anh Practical Accounting Training Center

Le Anh Practical Accounting Training Center is one of Vietnam's top prestigious and quality accounting training addresses. Established in 2012 with more than 10 years of experience, Le Anh Accounting Center is one of the few establishments licensed to operate in the field of accounting training.

Le Anh Accounting Center stands out for its exceptional delivery of practical accounting courses, impacting thousands of students nationwide across various provinces like Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang, Binh Duong, and beyond. The center's distinctiveness lies in its tailored approach to courses, offering a unique blend of hands-on learning experiences that resonate with diverse student needs.

These courses go beyond just teaching theoretical concepts; they also prioritize real-world applications. By emphasizing practical skills, they ensure that learners acquire the necessary tools for success in the accounting field.

The difference between Le Anh Accounting compared to other centers

Compared to other accounting training units, Le Anh Accounting Center has the following differences:

  • Le Anh Accounting Center is one of the few accounting centers licensed to provide training in the education sector.
  • Le Anh Center's training program is practical and updated according to trends. In addition, the teaching textbooks are compiled and built based on the practical experience of experts working in the fields of accounting and finance. Training content is always updated to meet labor market requirements.
  • Team of quality, experienced lecturers, 100% of the Center's lecturers are experts and chief accountants with over 10 years of practical experience.
  • Supporting students to find jobs after graduation, with the desire to support students, Le Anh Center through the HR department, supports connecting students with recruitment units.
  • Preferential tuition has not increased for many consecutive years. The Center commits to keep tuition the same for registered students for many consecutive years. Tuition fees are always kept stable at an optimal level.

Courses at Le Anh Accounting Center

The center offers a variety of accounting, finance, and tax courses suitable for all types of students from basic to advanced. Some typical courses include:

  • Practical General Accounting Course
  • Accounting Principles Course
  • Online General Accounting Course
  • Intensive Tax Accounting Course
  • Business Financial Analysis Course
  • Chief Accountant Certificate Course
  • Advanced Accounting Course (Best Seller)
  • Career Administrative Accounting Course
  • Accounting Course for Business Owners
  • Accounting Course for FDI Foreign Enterprises
  • Individual Business Household Accounting Course
  • Packaged Online Accounting Courses
  • Professional Human Resources Administration Course
  • Practical Import-Export Professional Course
  • Personal Financial Management Course

There are also certificate courses for chief accountants, FDI enterprise accounting, career administrative accounting, etc. to meet the diverse needs of students.

Support policies for students

When studying at Le Anh Accounting Center, students will enjoy the following support policies:

  • Learn until you become proficient and do real work.
  • Receive technical support and advice on accounting, finance, and taxes after the course.
  • Be introduced to suitable jobs after completing the course through the Le Anh HR department.
  • Received accounting software worth tens of millions to support study and work.

With preferential tuition policies and post-training support policies, Le Anh Accounting Center is committed to providing students with the best learning and training environment.
